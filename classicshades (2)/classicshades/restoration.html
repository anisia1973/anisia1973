Damage Categories & Magnitudes

Should your home become unsafe for habitation due to rising water and mold
presence, our team will come and inspect your property to determine the best course
of recovery. Using advanced tools and measuring methods, we first classify the damage
and threat level before taking action. Your property is classified into one of three
categories, depending on the type of water leak, how long it has been there, and what
areas or rooms are flooded. These categories include:

Catagory Stages Description
Category 1: An overflow of faucet water on a first floor only.
Catagory 2: A two-story home overflows from an upper level or water has become stagnate.
Category 3: When wastewater backflows and comes into contact with any surface, this is a
biohazard. In these cases, containment is top priority and any wood or tile
that has been damaged must be removed.
